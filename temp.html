Latitude vs Max Temperature Scatter Plot/h1>

As the city latitudes approach zero degrees (the equator), the temperature generally rises. While there are a few outliers where the max temperature peaks higher than at the equator, the general trend of higher temperatures close to the equator remains. Additionally, the southern hemisphere appears to have higher temepratures than the northern hemisphere.
